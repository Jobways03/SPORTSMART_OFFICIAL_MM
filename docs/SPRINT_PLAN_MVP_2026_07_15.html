The 3 ship-blockers

  1. Inventory write path — works in code review but never verified end-to-end with a real order. Sprint 1.
  2. COD UPI refunds get stuck in MANUAL_REQUIRED — finance touches every UPI refund manually. Unsustainable past week one. Sprint 1.
  3. Refund saga has no recovery cron — if a saga crashes mid-flight, the refund is silently lost. Sprint 1.

How a story moves through the sprint

It's not ready to start unless: the user story is written, acceptance criteria are testable, it's sized (1/2/3/5/8 points), dependencies are clear, and the PO has confirmed business value.

It's not done until: all acceptance criteria are met, code-reviewed by someone else, tests added, merged to main, deployed to staging, QA signed off, PO accepted, and no new CRITICAL/HIGH defects opened.
